Welcome to Al-Hayaat school

School vision statement
With constant mindfulness of Allah, the Most High, and the Hereafter, we strive to provide our students with the strongest foundation in a secure environment. Through exemplary role models and high-quality, Islamically-oriented education, we aim to empower them to navigate the world as confident, contributing Muslims. In doing so, we prioritize the character of the School above all else.
Al-Hayaat School is set to open its doors in 2026, initially offering classes from Junior Kindergarten to
Grade 3. We have ambitious plans to expand our grade offerings in the future..

Why Al-Hayaat?

Al-Hayaat School offers a unique educational experience that seamlessly integrates values with academic excellence. Here’s why we stand out:

Faith-based education

At Al-Hayaat School, our mission is deeply rooted in the teachings of the Quran and the principles of Ahlulbayt [a.s.]. We emphasize the importance of living a pure life "حَيِّاةٌ طَيِّبَةٌ", fostering qualities such as honesty, patience, respect, and compassion in our students.

Comprehensive curriculum

Our curriculum blends Ontario’s academic standards with studies, Quranic education, and Arabic language. This combination ensures studentsreceive a well-rounded education that nurtures intellectual growth while strengthening their identity.

Commitment to academic excellence

We provide a high-quality education that equips students with the tools they need to thrive in modern society. Our focus on intellectual development, physical health, and recreational activities ensures a holistic learning experience.

Community-focused vision

Al-Hayaat School is dedicated to fostering a nurturing environment where students grow into skilled professionals, compassionate leaders, and contributors to the Shia community and humanity at large.

Guidance from experienced educators

Our dedicated team of teachers, principal, and board of directors bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom. They are committed to equipping students with the skills and mindset needed to succeed in the modern era while remaining firmly grounded in their identity. Their collective efforts ensure that the curriculum not only meets academic standards but also prepares students to face the challenges of today’s dynamic world

By combining a strong academic foundation with guidance from experienced leaders, Al-Hayaat School is committed to nurturing students who excel in all aspects of life, contributing positively to their communities and beyond.

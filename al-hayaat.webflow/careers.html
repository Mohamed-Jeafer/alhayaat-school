Welcome to Al-Hayaat School

At Al-Hayaat School, our mission is to provide a well-rounded education rooted in the teachings of the Quran and Ahlulbayt [A.S.]. Guided by the principles of حَيَاةٌ طَيِّبَةٌ (pure life), we are dedicated to cultivating a conducive learning environment that nurtures values, academic excellence, and holistic development.Our school is set to open in 2026, and we are actively building a team of passionate educators and professionals to help bring our vision to life.

This is more than a job—it’s an opportunity to shape the next generation of leaders, innovators, and contributors to humanity and the community.

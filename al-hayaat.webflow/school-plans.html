School Plans

Al-Hayaat School is set to open its doors in September 2026, offering a faith-based, high-quality education for the Kitchener-Waterloo community. Our carefully phased growth plan ensures a strong foundation for the school’s future:
Set to open
  • 2026: Opening with Junior Kindergarten (JK) to Grade 3.
  • Annual Expansion: Adding one grade each year until reaching Grade 8 in 2031​
  • This gradual development allows us to focus on creating a robust academic and spiritual environment tailored to each age group.
